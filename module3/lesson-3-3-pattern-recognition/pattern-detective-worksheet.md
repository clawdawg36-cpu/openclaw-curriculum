### 🔍 Your Mission

You are a Pattern Detective. Each puzzle below has a hidden rule. Your job:
1. **Solve it yourself first** — write your answer and the rule you found
2. **Ask OpenClaw** (or watch the class demo) — record what it says
3. **Reflect** — did you find the same rule? Different answer? Why might that be?

> **Important:** Do NOT skip to OpenClaw first. Your brain matters too. Detectives think for themselves before comparing notes.
