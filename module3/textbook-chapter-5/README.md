**Grade Level:** 6–8  
**Module:** 3 — Knowledge and Reasoning  
**Type:** Companion Textbook Chapter

---

## Overview

Chapter 5 introduces the foundational concepts of AI reasoning: how AI represents the world as numbers (features and representation) and how decision trees allow AI to work through a problem step by step.

This chapter bridges the perception concepts from Module 2 (how AI sees and hears) to the reasoning concepts at the heart of Module 3.
