## How This Lab Works

1. **Read each challenge carefully.**
2. **Solve it yourself first** — write your answer in the "My Answer" box before touching OpenClaw.
3. **Ask OpenClaw using the Chain-of-Thought prompt** (see template below).
4. **Record OpenClaw's answer and reasoning** (copy the key parts).
5. **Compare and reflect.** Mark the outcome:
   - ✅ OpenClaw got it right
   - ❌ OpenClaw got it wrong
   - 🤔 Right answer, wrong or shaky reasoning
6. Write a **1–2 sentence reflection** for each.
