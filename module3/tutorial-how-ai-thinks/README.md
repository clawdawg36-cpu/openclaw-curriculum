## Coverage

1. **Features & Representation** — How AI converts real-world things into numbers (feature vectors, representation learning)
2. **Decision Trees** — The simplest interpretable form of AI reasoning
3. **Pattern Recognition** — Finding rules in data automatically (supervised learning)
4. **Where AI Succeeds & Fails** — Strengths and known failure modes
5. **Chain-of-Thought** — How to ask AI to show its work; why it dramatically improves accuracy
6. **Lab Preview** — "In the lab, you'll give your agent a reasoning skill it doesn't have yet"
