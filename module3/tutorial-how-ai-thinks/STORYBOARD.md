**On-Screen Text:**
```
Chain-of-Thought = ask AI to show its steps
"Think step by step" → dramatically better accuracy
```

**Animation Notes:**
- Text streams in on right side like a typewriter
- Steps appear one at a time as narration says each one
- Final answer: bold green glow, slight scale-up for emphasis

**Accessibility Alt Text:**
"Split screen. Left: AI is asked 17 times 24 and gives a wrong answer instantly. Right: AI is asked the same question with 'think step by step.' It shows three intermediate steps, then correctly answers 408."
