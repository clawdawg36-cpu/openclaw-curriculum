# Online Tutorial — Welcome to OpenClaw

**Module:** 1 — What Is AI? — Meet Your Digital Brain  
**Format:** Video tutorial (~5 minutes)  
**Used In:** Lesson 1.3 (assign as pre-work or in-class intro)  
**Audience:** Students with zero prior AI assistant experience

## Overview

A short introductory video tutorial that prepares students for their first OpenClaw interaction. Covers what OpenClaw is, how to start a conversation, what kinds of questions to ask, what personal information to never share, and how to critically evaluate AI responses rather than accepting them at face value.

## Content Sections (~30 sec each)

1. What is OpenClaw and why we're using it
2. How to start your first conversation
3. Types of questions you can ask (factual, creative, opinion)
4. Privacy — what NOT to share (name, address, school, passwords)
5. Evaluating AI responses — the 5-point critical checklist
6. Live demo walkthrough
7. Quick tips for getting better responses
8. Wrap-up and what's coming next
