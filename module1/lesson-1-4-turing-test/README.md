# Lesson 1.4 — The Turing Test & AI Limits

**Module:** 1 — What Is AI? — Meet Your Digital Brain  
**Duration:** 1 class period (~50 min)  
**Sequence:** Fourth and final lesson of Module 1

## Overview

Students explore the famous Turing Test and grapple with what it means for a machine to be "intelligent." They play **"Is It Human?"** — reading pairs of responses (one AI-generated, one human-written) and trying to tell the difference. The discussion digs into AI's genuine capabilities and real limitations, setting up the transition to Module 2's focus on perception.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:
- Explain the Turing Test and its significance in AI history
- Identify at least 3 things AI does well and 3 things it struggles with
- Articulate why passing the Turing Test may or may not indicate true intelligence
- Connect AI limitations to the broader question of what "thinking" means
