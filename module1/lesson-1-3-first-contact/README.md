# Lesson 1.3 — First Contact with OpenClaw

**Module:** 1 — What Is AI? — Meet Your Digital Brain  
**Duration:** 1–2 class periods (~50–100 min)  
**Sequence:** Third lesson of Module 1

## Overview

Students have their first hands-on interaction with an AI assistant (OpenClaw). After a brief setup walkthrough, they work through the **OpenClaw 101 Lab** — 10 structured challenges designed to explore the full range of what AI can and can't do. The lesson closes with a reflection journal entry documenting surprises, confusions, and questions.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:
- Successfully initiate and carry on a conversation with an AI assistant
- Ask three types of questions (factual, creative, opinion-based) and compare the responses
- Identify at least 2 things AI does well and 2 things it struggles with
- Practice safe and responsible AI use (privacy, critical evaluation)
