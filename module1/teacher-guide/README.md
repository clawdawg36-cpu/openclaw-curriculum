# Teacher Guide — Module 1

**Module:** 1 — What Is AI? — Meet Your Digital Brain  
**Audience:** Classroom teachers, including those without CS or AI background  
**Length:** ~1,000 lines — comprehensive reference document

## Overview

The complete teacher guide for Module 1. Written for educators who may be teaching AI concepts for the first time. Covers everything from setup and pacing to handling tricky student questions and communicating with parents.

## Note for First-Time AI Teachers

You don't need to be an AI expert to teach this module. Students are often ahead of teachers in using AI tools — lean into that. Your job is to facilitate critical thinking, not deliver technical lectures. The facilitation guide in Section 5 has everything you need.
