# Module 1: What Is AI?

**Grade Level:** 6–8  
**Duration:** ~3 weeks (15 class periods)  
**Prerequisites:** None — this is the entry point!

---

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students will be able to:
- Define artificial intelligence in their own words
- Identify examples of AI in everyday life
- Distinguish between AI, automation, and human intelligence
- Have their first conversation with an AI assistant (OpenClaw)
- Explain the basic concept of the Turing Test
- Demonstrate responsible, curious engagement with AI tools
