# Lesson 1.1 — What Is AI?

**Module:** 1 — What Is AI? — Meet Your Digital Brain  
**Duration:** 1 class period (~50 min)  
**Sequence:** First lesson of Module 1

## Overview

Students are introduced to artificial intelligence for the first time. The lesson opens with a philosophical warm-up ("What makes something intelligent?"), walks through the history of AI from Alan Turing to ChatGPT, and introduces the AI4K12 Five Big Ideas framework that will anchor the entire curriculum.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:
- Define "artificial intelligence" in their own words
- Identify at least 3 key milestones in AI history
- Name the AI4K12 Five Big Ideas and give a one-sentence description of each
- Distinguish between AI systems and traditional software
