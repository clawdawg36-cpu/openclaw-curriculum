## 🏆 Bingo Rules

- **Fill each square** with the name of a real AI system that fits the category
- **No repeats** — each AI system can only be used once
- **Rows count** horizontally, vertically, or diagonally
- **Call Bingo!** when you complete a row, then read your examples aloud
- **Bonus:** Fill the whole card for a "Blackout Bingo" win!
