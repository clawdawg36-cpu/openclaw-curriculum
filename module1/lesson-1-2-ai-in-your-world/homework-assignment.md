## What Counts as AI?

AI systems often:
- Learn from data (they get better over time)
- Make predictions or recommendations
- Recognize patterns (faces, voices, images, text)
- Make decisions automatically

Examples: recommendation engines, spam filters, voice assistants, navigation apps, face unlock, autocorrect, fitness trackers, content feeds.

## Tips for Finding AI

- 📱 **Your phone:** Camera (face recognition), keyboard (autocorrect/predictive text), voice assistant, photo organization
- 🎵 **Music & video:** Spotify recommendations, YouTube autoplay, TikTok For You page
- 🛒 **Shopping:** Product recommendations on Amazon, price tracking tools
- 🗺️ **Getting around:** Google Maps, Waze traffic predictions
- 🏥 **Health & fitness:** Fitness trackers, symptom checkers, mental health apps
- 🎮 **Games:** NPC behavior, matchmaking systems, difficulty adjustment
- 📧 **Communication:** Email spam filter, Google Smart Reply, auto-translate
- 🏠 **At home:** Smart thermostat, security cameras, streaming TV
- 🏫 **At school:** Plagiarism detectors, adaptive learning tools, essay feedback
