# Lesson 1.2 — AI in Your World

**Module:** 1 — What Is AI? — Meet Your Digital Brain  
**Duration:** 1 class period (~50 min)  
**Sequence:** Second lesson of Module 1

## Overview

Students discover how pervasive AI already is in their everyday lives. After debriefing a take-home observation assignment, they play **AI Bingo** — finding real-world AI examples across 15 categories. The lesson closes with a structured discussion: which AI systems surprised them, which they'd miss most, and which seem potentially risky.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:
- Identify AI systems across at least 5 different categories of everyday life
- Explain in plain language what each identified system does
- Begin to form opinions about the benefits and risks of AI in society
