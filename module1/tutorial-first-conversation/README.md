# Online Tutorial — Your First OpenClaw Conversation

**Module:** 1 — What Is AI? — Meet Your Digital Brain  
**Format:** Interactive step-by-step walkthrough (not a video)  
**Used In:** Lesson 1.3 (in-class or as homework)  
**Audience:** Students completing their first guided AI conversation

## Overview

A 6-step interactive tutorial that walks students through their first structured OpenClaw conversation. Unlike the Welcome video (which is passive), this tutorial requires students to actually interact with OpenClaw at each step and record their responses. Designed to run in a browser alongside OpenClaw.

## The 6 Steps

| Step | Action | Purpose |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Introduce yourself | Break the ice, see how AI responds to personal context |
| 2 | Ask a factual question | Explore knowledge accuracy |
| 3 | Ask a creative question | Explore generative capabilities |
| 4 | Ask for help with a task | Explore practical utility |
| 5 | Ask something it might not know | Discover limitations |
| 6 | Reflect: what surprised you? | Build metacognitive awareness |

Each step includes a tutorial prompt, sample starter prompts for students who are stuck, and an inline reflection question before moving on.

## Implementation Options

- **In-class:** Students work through on their own device, teacher circulates (20–25 min)
- **Homework:** Assign after Lesson 1.3, discuss responses next class as warm-up
- **Paired:** Students compare their AI responses — great for discussion about consistency
