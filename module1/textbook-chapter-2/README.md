# Companion Textbook — Chapter 2: AI Through History — From Turing to Today

**Module:** 1 — What Is AI? — Meet Your Digital Brain
**Reading Level:** Grades 6–8 (Flesch-Kincaid 6th–7th grade)
**Length:** ~750 lines (~39KB) — full first draft

## Overview

Chapter 2 of the *AI Explorers* companion textbook. A deep-dive history of AI from Alan Turing's 1950 paper through the 2020s generative AI era. Written in a narrative style with multiple "AI in the Wild" sidebars and profiling key figures. Each section connects historical content to the present — and to the student's own experience using AI tools.

## Connection to Chapter 1

Chapter 1 includes a brief timeline of AI history (Turing → AlphaGo → GPT era). Chapter 2 expands this into a full narrative, revisiting those same milestones with much more depth. Students who read both will see the same arc from two different distances — useful for reinforcement.
