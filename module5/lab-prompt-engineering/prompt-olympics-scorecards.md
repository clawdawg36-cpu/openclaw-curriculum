---

## How Prompt Olympics Works

1. Each team writes a prompt for the event
2. Teams submit prompts to the AI at the same time
3. **Judges** score each AI response (not the prompt — the *result*)
4. Scores are tallied and a winner is declared
5. Teams share their winning prompts and explain their strategy

**Roles:** 1 Prompt Writer · 1 AI Runner · 2–3 Judges (rotate each event)
