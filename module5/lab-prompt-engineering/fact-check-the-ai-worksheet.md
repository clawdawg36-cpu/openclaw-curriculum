## Why Fact-Check AI?

AI systems — including OpenClaw — can say things that sound confident and correct but are actually wrong, outdated, or exaggerated. This is sometimes called a **hallucination**: the AI generates text that sounds plausible but isn't accurate.

**Good AI users verify important claims before trusting them.**

In this activity, you'll investigate 10 claims about OpenClaw and AI — some true, some false, some "it depends." Use any reliable source to check each one: official documentation, your teacher, credible websites, or your own testing.

| Source | Type | Why trustworthy? |

| Wikipedia (for general AI concepts) | Reference | Good starting point, always check citations |
| Published news articles | Secondary | Check the date — AI moves fast! |
| Your own testing | Empirical | You saw it yourself |
