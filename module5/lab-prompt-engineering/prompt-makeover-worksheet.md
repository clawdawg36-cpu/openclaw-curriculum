## What's a Prompt Makeover?

A weak prompt gets vague, boring, or useless results. A strong prompt gives the AI clear instructions, context, and constraints — and gets you exactly what you need.

**Your job:** Transform each weak prompt into a powerful one, then run both and compare results.

**The Golden Rules of a Strong Prompt:**
- 📌 **Context** — Who/what is this for?
- 🎯 **Goal** — What exactly do you want?
- 📐 **Format** — How should it be structured?
- 🚧 **Constraints** — Length, tone, audience, restrictions?
- 💡 **Examples** — Show the AI what "good" looks like (optional but powerful)
