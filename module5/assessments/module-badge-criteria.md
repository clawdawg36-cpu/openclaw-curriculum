# Module 5 Badge Criteria
## 🏅 Prompt Pro Badge

**Awarded to students who demonstrate mastery of AI prompt engineering and channel fundamentals.**

---

## Badge Overview

| | |
|---|---|
| **Badge Name** | Prompt Pro |
| **Module** | 5 — Talking to AI |
| **Type** | Mastery Badge |
| **Prerequisites** | Module 4 completion recommended |

---

## Earning the Badge

Students must complete **both** requirements:

---

### Requirement 1: Complete 10 of 15 Prompt Challenges ✅

Throughout Module 5, students encounter **15 prompt challenges** spread across lessons and labs. These are marked with a 🎯 icon in lesson materials.

Students must successfully complete at least **10 of 15** to earn the badge.

**What counts as "complete":**
- Prompt submitted (not just attempted)
- Output received and reviewed
- Brief note included explaining what worked or what they'd change

**Prompt Challenges by Lesson:**

| Lesson | Challenges |
|--------|-----------|
| 5-1: How Chatbots Work | 🎯 Challenges 1–2 |
| 5-2: Art of the Prompt | 🎯 Challenges 3–6 |
| 5-3: Prompt Olympics | 🎯 Challenges 7–9 |
| 5-4: AI Study Partner | 🎯 Challenges 10–11 |
| 5-5: When AI Gets It Wrong | 🎯 Challenges 12–13 |
| 5-6: How Channels Work | 🎯 Challenges 14–15 |

*Students track their own completions on their prompt challenge log (provided in lesson materials).*

---

### Requirement 2: Pass the Module 5 Quiz ✅

**Passing score: 70% or higher (7/10 questions)**

Students may retake the quiz **once** after reviewing the vocabulary list and lesson materials.

**Quiz covers:**
- Vocabulary definitions (tokens, context window, hallucination, API, webhook, etc.)
- Prompt technique identification
- Recognizing hallucinations vs. accurate AI output
- Basic channel connection concepts

---

## Badge Tiers

| Tier | Requirements | Visual |
|------|-------------|--------|
| **Prompt Pro** | 10/15 challenges + quiz pass | 🏅 Standard badge |
| **Prompt Pro Gold** | 15/15 challenges + quiz 90%+ | 🥇 Gold badge |
| **Prompt Pro + Olympic Medalist** | Badge + top-3 Prompt Olympics finish | 🏅 + 🥇/🥈/🥉 |

---

## How Students Claim Their Badge

1. Complete prompt challenge log (mark off completed challenges)
2. Pass the quiz (teacher records score)
3. Submit challenge log to teacher for verification
4. Teacher awards badge in class tracking system / gradebook

---

## Teacher Notes

**Tracking options:**
- Paper challenge log (included in lesson materials)
- Google Sheet tracker (template available in teacher resources)
- Class Dojo / other badge system of your choice

**Retake policy:**
- Quiz: 1 retake allowed, no time limit
- Challenges: Students may revisit and redo challenges — what matters is completion, not first attempt

**Displaying badges:**
- Print-and-clip badge cards for physical notebooks or folders
- Digital badge image for student portfolios or slideshow
- Consider a classroom "Wall of Prompt Pros" for motivation

**This badge can serve as:**
- Extra credit bonus
- Portfolio requirement
- Entry ticket for Module 6 advanced activities
