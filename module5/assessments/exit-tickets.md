## Teacher Notes for Exit Tickets
- Collect before students leave (paper, form, or sticky note version both work)
- Quick-scan the traffic lights to identify who needs check-ins the next day
- Don't grade these — use them as formative data only
- Consider sharing interesting (anonymized) answers the next day to start discussion
