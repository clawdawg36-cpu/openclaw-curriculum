## Using This for Demo Planning

✅ = **Candidate for your demo.** These are ready to show live.  
⚠️ = **Show with a warning,** or use a screenshot/video fallback.  
❌ = **Don't demo.** Mention it during Build if relevant, but don't show a broken feature.

**Pro tip:** Demoing something broken is worse than acknowledging it's not finished. Your audience respects honesty.
