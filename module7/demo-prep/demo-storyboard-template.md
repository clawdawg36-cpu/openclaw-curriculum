## How to Use This Template

Each panel = a section of your 5-minute demo. Sketch or describe what's on screen, what you're saying, and what you want the audience to *feel* at that moment. There are no wrong answers — this is your personal planning tool.

---

## Quick Checklist Before You Rehearse

- [ ] I know exactly what I'll show in the Hook (30 sec)
- [ ] I can tell my build story without looking at notes
- [ ] I've tested my 3 demo inputs and they work
- [ ] I have a backup plan if the demo breaks
- [ ] I've written my reflection (honest, specific, not vague)
- [ ] Total time ≈ 5 minutes when I run through it
