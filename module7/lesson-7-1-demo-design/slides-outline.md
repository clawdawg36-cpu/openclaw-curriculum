## Slide 3: The Stakes
**You Have 5 Minutes.**  
What do you want them to feel when it's over?

- Not: "That was technically impressive."
- Yes: "I want to try that." / "I didn't know that was possible." / "Wait, they BUILT that?"

## Slide 4: The Framework
**The Hook-Build-Demo-Reflect Arc**

| Section | Time | Purpose |
|---------|------|---------|
| 🎣 Hook | 30 sec | Make them care |
| 🏗️ Build | 2 min | Show the journey |
| 🖥️ Demo | 2 min | Show don't tell |
| 🪞 Reflect | 30 sec | Earn their trust |

---

## Slide 5: The Hook
**Start With the Surprise**

❌ "Hi, my name is... and today I'm going to talk about..."  
✅ [Show your agent doing something cool. First 30 seconds. No intro.]

*The test: Would someone who walked in late still be curious?*

---

## Slide 6: The Build
**Tell the Story of How It Was Built**

- M1: Name + personality
- M2: Perception (vision/audio)
- M3: Domain knowledge
- M4: Trained model
- M5: Live channel
- M6: Ethics + guardrails

*20–30 seconds per milestone. Keep moving.*

---

## Slide 7: The Demo
**Live. Real Inputs. Not Screenshots.**

✅ Use interesting, real inputs  
✅ Demo 3+ capabilities  
✅ Have a backup plan (screenshots) if it breaks  
❌ Don't type "test" or "hello world"  
❌ Don't apologize if something glitches — narrate it

---

## Slide 8: The Reflect
**Honesty Is the Most Impressive Thing**

"Here's what my agent doesn't do well yet..."  
"If I had more time, I would..."  
"I built this. It does [X, Y, Z]."

*Don't end with an apology. End with a statement.*
