# Pre-Expo Technical Checklist
**Module 7 — AI Expo Preparation**
**Format:** Digital interactive checklist (printable backup version included below)

---

> **How to use this checklist:**
> Complete every item before your AI Expo demo. Work through it top to bottom — each section builds on the last. Mark items ✅ as you go. Do not skip items. If something is broken, fix it before Expo day.

---

## Section 1: Your Agent Is Running

- [ ] **My agent is running and responding.**
  Open your agent interface and send it a test message. Did it respond? If yes, check this box.

- [ ] **My agent responds to at least 3 different inputs.**
  Test a text question, an image input (if you built that), and something in its knowledge domain. All three work? Check.

- [ ] **My agent's personality comes through.**
  Does it sound like *your* agent — not just a generic AI? Re-read its responses. Tweak if needed.

- [ ] **I know my agent's name and can explain it to someone who's never seen it.**
  Practice saying: "My agent is called [name]. It's designed to [one sentence]. Here's what it can do."

---

## Section 2: Your Channel Is Connected

- [ ] **My agent is connected to the live channel I plan to demo on.**
  (Example: BlueBubbles, Discord, a web interface.) Send a message through that channel right now. Did it come through?

- [ ] **My channel works on the device I'm presenting from.**
  On the day of Expo, you'll use a specific laptop, phone, or tablet. Test on *that* device — not just your usual one.

- [ ] **I have the login credentials I need, ready to go.**
  Don't count on remembering passwords on demo day. Write them down or save them somewhere you can access quickly.

- [ ] **Wi-Fi is confirmed at the Expo location (or I have a hotspot as backup).**
  If you're not sure about the venue's Wi-Fi, plan to use your phone as a hotspot. Test that your agent works on your hotspot *now*.

---

## Section 3: Your Backup Plan Is Ready

- [ ] **I have a 60-second backup video of my agent working.**
  This is a screen recording of your agent doing its 3 key capabilities. Saved locally — not just in the cloud.
  - Record on: ______________________
  - Saved to: ______________________

- [ ] **I have 3–4 screenshots of my best agent moments.**
  One screenshot per major capability. Clear, high-resolution, easy to show on a projected screen.

- [ ] **My backup video and screenshots are accessible without internet.**
  Try airplane mode. Can you open the video? Can you pull up the screenshots? If not, fix this now.

- [ ] **I have a simple slide or PDF with my backup screenshots.**
  Even just three slides: one per capability. Saved on the device I'm presenting from.

- [ ] **I've practiced what I'll say if the live demo fails.**
  Out loud. At least once. The line is: *"My internet seems to be having trouble — let me show you the backup video I prepared."* No apology. No panic. Just a pivot.

---

## Section 4: Your Narrative Is Ready

- [ ] **I can deliver my Hook without looking at notes.**
  The Hook is your first 30 seconds. It's your agent doing something surprising. Practice it until it's second nature.

- [ ] **I can walk through the Build in 2 minutes.**
  Module by module: what you added and why. Time yourself. Trim if you're going over.

- [ ] **I know which 3 capabilities I'm demoing live.**
  Write them here:
  1. ______________________
  2. ______________________
  3. ______________________

- [ ] **I can deliver my Reflect without it feeling like an apology.**
  Practice saying what your agent gets wrong in a confident, matter-of-fact tone. This makes you look *more* credible, not less.

- [ ] **I've timed my full demo at least once.**
  Full run-through, start to finish, with timer. Did you land near 5 minutes? If you're over, what cuts can you make?

---

## Section 5: On Expo Day

- [ ] **I arrived early enough to set up and test.**
  Aim for 15 minutes before your slot. Plug in, open your agent, test one quick message.

- [ ] **I've confirmed the Wi-Fi or hotspot works at this location.**
  Not just connected — actually tested by sending a message to your agent.

- [ ] **My backup video is still accessible on this device.**
  One last check. Open it. Does it play?

- [ ] **I know what to do if something breaks mid-demo.**
  Stay calm. Pivot to backup. Say the line. Keep going.

- [ ] **I'm ready to answer questions honestly.**
  You don't need to know everything. You built something real. Own what you know; acknowledge what you don't.

---

## Quick Reference: If Things Go Wrong

| Problem | What to do |
|---------|------------|
| Wi-Fi drops | Switch to hotspot; if no hotspot, go to backup video |
| Agent won't respond | Say "let me try one more time" — try once. Then pivot to backup. |
| Wrong response / weird output | Laugh it off if you can: "This is actually a great example of where AI still has work to do." Then continue. |
| Total system failure | Go straight to backup video. No apology. No long explanation. Just say: "Let me show you the prepared demo." |
| Forget what to say | Look at your checklist (this one). Take a breath. Start with your Hook. |
