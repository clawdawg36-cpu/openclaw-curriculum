# Student Choice Awards
## AI Expo — Module 7

*Voted by the class. Announced at the end of the Expo.*

---

## How It Works

1. Every student votes on a ballot (see below)
2. Teacher (or designated students) tallies votes
3. Awards announced at the end of the Expo day
4. Winners get a shout-out — and bragging rights

*Rules: You cannot vote for yourself. You must watch at least two presentations before voting.*

---

## The Awards

### 🏆 Most Surprising Capability
*For the agent that did something nobody expected*

> Recognizes: creativity, ambition, or a capability that made the audience say "wait — it can do THAT?"

---

### 🗣️ Best Explanation for a Non-Tech Person
*For the presenter who made it click for everyone in the room*

> Recognizes: clarity, great analogies, patience, and the skill of translating technical work into human language.

---

### 🪞 Most Honest Reflection
*For the presenter who told the real story — including what went wrong*

> Recognizes: intellectual courage, self-awareness, and the kind of honesty that actually teaches us something.

## Teacher Note

In case of a tie, teacher discretion applies. You can co-award or pick based on your own observation of the Expo. The goal is celebrating the right things — not creating winners and losers.
