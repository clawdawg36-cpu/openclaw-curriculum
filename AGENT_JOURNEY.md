# 🗺️ Your Agent Journey

### *Build Your AI Assistant, Piece by Piece*

---

> You're not just learning about AI — you're **building one**.
> Each module unlocks a new superpower for your personal AI agent.
> By Module 7, you'll stand in front of an audience and demo something you made from scratch.
>
> *That's the journey. Let's go.*

### 🐣 MODULE 1 — Deploy Your Agent
**Capability Unlock: Identity**

```
[ ] Complete
```

**What you build:** Your agent comes to life. You deploy OpenClaw, give it a name,
write its personality, and verify it's working.

**Your agent gains:**
- ✅ A name and identity
- ✅ A custom personality / system prompt
- ✅ The ability to have a conversation

**Key Lab:** `lab-deploy-your-openclaw` — set up your OpenClaw instance

**Agent Status After M1:**

```
   Can: Have a conversation

```

### 👁️ MODULE 2 — Give Your Agent Vision
**Capability Unlock: Perception**

```
[ ] Complete
```

**What you build:** You connect your agent to Hugging Face image and audio models.
Give it an image — it can describe it. Play it a sound — it can identify it.

**Your agent gains:**
- ✅ Image understanding (computer vision)
- ✅ Audio recognition
- ✅ Multimodal perception — your agent senses more than just text

**Key Lab:** Connect a Hugging Face model as a custom OpenClaw skill

**Agent Status After M2:**
```
🤖 [YOUR AGENT NAME]
   Can: Have a conversation, describe images, identify audio
   Can't yet: Know custom things, learn, speak out loud, or behave ethically by design
```

---

### 🧠 MODULE 3 — Give Your Agent a Knowledge Base
**Capability Unlock: Knowledge**

```
[ ] Complete
```

**What you build:** You write a custom OpenClaw skill that gives your agent specific knowledge
and reasoning abilities. What does YOUR agent know that others don't?

**Your agent gains:**
- ✅ A custom skill with domain knowledge
- ✅ Reasoning logic (if/then chains, lookups, decisions)
- ✅ Something unique that YOU put into it

**Key Lab:** Write your first SKILL.md and deploy it to your OpenClaw instance

**Agent Status After M3:**
```
🤖 [YOUR AGENT NAME]
   Can: Have a conversation, see images, identify audio, use custom knowledge
   Can't yet: Learn from new data, speak out loud, or behave ethically by design
```

---

### 📚 MODULE 4 — Give Your Agent the Ability to Learn
**Capability Unlock: Learning**

```
[ ] Complete
```

**What you build:** You train a real machine learning model on a dataset you choose,
then integrate it into your agent. Your agent now learns from examples — not just rules.

**Your agent gains:**
- ✅ A trained model (your own!)
- ✅ The ability to improve with data
- ✅ Understanding of training, validation, and model accuracy

**Key Lab:** Train a model with Teachable Machine or a simple Python notebook; integrate it

**Agent Status After M4:**
```
🤖 [YOUR AGENT NAME]
   Can: Converse, see, hear, use custom knowledge, classify/predict from a trained model
   Can't yet: Speak out loud to people, or behave ethically by design
```

---

### 🎙️ MODULE 5 — Give Your Agent a Voice
**Capability Unlock: Communication**

```
[ ] Complete
```

**What you build:** You connect your agent to a real communication channel —
Discord, iMessage, or another platform. Your agent can now reach out to people in the real world.

**Your agent gains:**
- ✅ A live communication channel
- ✅ The ability to receive and reply to messages
- ✅ Real-world presence beyond the terminal

**Key Lab:** Configure an OpenClaw channel plugin; test end-to-end messaging

**Agent Status After M5:**
```
🤖 [YOUR AGENT NAME]
   Can: Converse, see, hear, know things, learn, AND message people in real life
   Can't yet: Behave ethically by design
```

---

### ⚖️ MODULE 6 — Make Your Agent Ethical
**Capability Unlock: Responsibility**

```
[ ] Complete
```

**What you build:** You add guardrails, bias checks, and a usage policy to your agent.
You decide what your agent should and shouldn't do — and enforce it.

**Your agent gains:**
- ✅ A written usage policy (what's allowed and what isn't)
- ✅ A bias audit of its training data and outputs
- ✅ Guardrail logic — behaviors your agent refuses to do

**Key Lab:** Audit your agent's outputs for bias; write and deploy a usage policy

**Agent Status After M6:**
```
🤖 [YOUR AGENT NAME]
   Can: Everything above — AND it does so responsibly, with policies you designed
   Status: COMPLETE ✅ — ready for the AI Expo
```

---

### 🎓 MODULE 7 — Capstone: AI Expo
**Capability Unlock: Presentation**

```
[ ] Complete
```

**What you build:** You present your finished agent to a real audience — classmates,
teachers, parents, community members. You explain what it does, how you built it,
and what you'd do differently next time.

**Your agent is:**
- ✅ Named and personalized
- ✅ Capable of vision and audio
- ✅ Equipped with custom knowledge
- ✅ Trained on real data
- ✅ Connected to a live channel
- ✅ Governed by ethical guardrails

**Key Deliverable:** A 5-minute demo + Q&A at the AI Expo
