## Vocabulary Check

*Match each term to its definition. (These were on your scenario sheet — check your notes.)*

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| ___ Surveillance | A. When a policy harms some groups more than others |
| ___ Chilling effect | B. Agreeing to something with full understanding |
| ___ Algorithmic bias | C. Systematic monitoring of people's behavior |
| ___ Disparate impact | D. When AI produces unfair results for certain groups |
| ___ Informed consent | E. When surveillance causes people to change behavior out of fear |

*(Answers: Surveillance = C, Chilling effect = E, Algorithmic bias = D, Disparate impact = A, Informed consent = B)*
