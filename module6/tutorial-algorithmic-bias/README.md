# Online Tutorial — What Is Algorithmic Bias?

**Module:** 6 — AI and Society: Who Benefits, Who Gets Left Behind  
**Format:** Video tutorial (~5 minutes)  
**Used In:** Lesson 6.2 (follows Tutorial A; can also stand alone)  
**Audience:** Students with basic AI familiarity; no technical background required

## Overview

A focused, approachable explainer on algorithmic bias. Uses the Amazon hiring screener (2018) as the anchor case study. Explains proxy variables at a 6th-grade level. Ends with concrete actions students can take. Designed to be direct, honest, and empowering — not scary.

## Content Sections (~60 sec each)

1. Hook — what is bias? (a quick, relatable definition)
2. How bias gets into AI (the short version)
3. Case study: Amazon's AI hiring screener
4. What's a proxy variable? (6th-grade explanation)
5. Why this matters to you — what YOU can do
