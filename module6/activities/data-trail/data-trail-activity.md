## 🕵️ Where Does Your Data Go?

You generate data constantly — even when you think you're just living your life. Every app you open, every search you type, every location you pass through — it all creates a trail.

Today, you're going to trace every data point you generate in a single school day. Then you'll think about who collects it, who might use it, and whether you're okay with that.

## 📌 Key Terms

**Data broker:** A company that buys and sells personal data — often data they didn't collect themselves.

**FERPA:** Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act — a law that protects students' educational records.

**COPPA:** Children's Online Privacy Protection Act — protects children under 13 from having their data collected without parental consent.

**Metadata:** Data about data. Example: not the content of your text, but who you texted, when, and for how long.

**Third-party sharing:** When a company shares your data with another company — often buried in Terms of Service.
