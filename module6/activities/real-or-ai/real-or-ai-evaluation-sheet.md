## 🔍 Can You Tell the Difference?

AI can now generate text that sounds human and images that look real. But there are often clues — if you know what to look for.

**Your challenge:** For each example, decide if it was created by a human or by AI. Then explain your reasoning. Finally, you'll find out the answer.

**This isn't just about being right.** It's about understanding *how* you make these judgments — and what that tells you about the limits of AI detection.

---

## 🧠 What to Look For

**In TEXT — possible AI signals:**
- Very smooth, "balanced" language that never commits to a strong opinion
- Long sentences that sound fluent but feel hollow
- Unusual repetition of phrases or ideas
- Perfect grammar with no personality or voice
- Vague generalizations instead of specific details
- Responds to a question but never really answers it

**In IMAGES — possible AI signals:**
- Hands with extra or fused fingers
- Eyes that don't quite match
- Text in the image that is garbled or nonsensical
- Hair or edges that look "melted" or blurry
- Lighting that doesn't match between subject and background
- Backgrounds that repeat or have strange geometry
