## 🗺️ Who Wins and Who Loses from an AI System?

Every AI system affects different groups of people in different ways. Some people benefit. Some are harmed. Some have no idea the system even exists — but it still affects them.

A **stakeholder** is anyone who is affected by or has an interest in a system.

Your job: map out ALL the stakeholders for the AI system in your case study, then analyze who gains and who loses.
