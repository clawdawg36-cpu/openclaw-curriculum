# Usage Policy Template
## [M6] Lab — Audit and Govern Your Agent

**Instructions:** Fill in this template to write a formal usage policy for your agent. Replace everything in *[brackets]* with your own words. Use the sentence starters as a guide — you can keep them or rewrite them.

A good usage policy is:
- **Specific** — not "won't do bad things" but "won't write content that targets a specific person"
- **Honest** — if your agent has limits, say so. Users deserve to know.
- **Clear** — a 7th grader should be able to read it
