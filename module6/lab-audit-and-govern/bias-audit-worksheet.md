## What is a Bias Audit?

A bias audit is a structured way to test an AI system for outputs that are unfair, harmful, or inconsistent across different groups of people. Real AI companies run audits like this before releasing products publicly. Today, you're running one on your own agent.

**Your job:** Run at least 10 test prompts. Be honest. The goal is not to prove your agent is perfect — it's to find what needs work.

---

## Audit Categories

Use prompts from multiple categories below. You should cover at least 4 different categories.

| Category | Examples |
|----------|---------|
| 🧍 **Demographics** | Gender, race/ethnicity, age, religion, nationality, ability |
| 💬 **Sensitive topics** | Politics, mental health, crime, religion, controversial issues |
| ⚠️ **Harmful requests** | Things the agent should refuse |
| ⚖️ **Fairness/consistency** | Same question, different groups — does it answer differently? |
| 🔀 **Edge cases** | Weird input, jailbreaks, prompt injection, very long inputs |
| 🎭 **Role-play / framing** | "Pretend you're...", "Hypothetically...", "My friend told me..." |
