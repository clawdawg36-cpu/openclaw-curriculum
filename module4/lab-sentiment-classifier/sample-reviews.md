## Training Examples (Add these to ML for Kids)

### POSITIVE Reviews

1. This movie was absolutely fantastic! I loved every minute of it.
2. A masterpiece. One of the best films I've ever seen.
3. Incredible performances and a gripping story from start to finish.
4. I was on the edge of my seat the whole time — brilliant!
5. Totally worth it. I'm recommending this to everyone I know.
6. The special effects were breathtaking and the plot kept me guessing.
7. Funny, heartwarming, and surprisingly emotional. A must-see!
8. One of those rare films that actually lives up to the hype.
9. The acting was superb and the soundtrack was beautiful.
10. I walked out of the theater feeling inspired. Amazing film.
11. An instant classic. Perfect pacing, perfect cast, perfect ending.
12. I've never laughed so hard in a theater. Absolutely hilarious.
13. This is exactly what cinema should be. Stunning and thought-provoking.
14. Best action sequences I've ever witnessed. Non-stop entertainment.
15. A beautiful love story that had me smiling for hours afterward.
16. Flawless from beginning to end. Standing ovation material.
17. The director created something truly special here. Don't miss it.
18. I went in with low expectations and left completely blown away.
19. Every performance was Oscar-worthy. What a talented cast.
20. I'm still thinking about this film three days later. Powerful stuff.

### NEGATIVE Reviews

1. What a waste of time. I walked out halfway through.
2. Terrible acting and a plot full of holes. Don't bother.
3. The worst movie I've seen this year. Completely disappointing.
4. Boring, predictable, and way too long. I nearly fell asleep.
5. The dialogue was cringeworthy and the ending made no sense.
6. A cheap, lazy sequel that disrespects the original.
7. Nothing happened for the first hour. Total snoozefest.
8. The CGI looked fake and the jokes all fell flat.
9. I want my money back. Truly awful experience.
10. Poorly written, poorly directed, and poorly acted. Avoid.
11. The trailer was better than the actual movie. False advertising.
12. An incoherent mess. I have no idea what the director was going for.
13. The worst script I've encountered in years. Painfully bad dialogue.
14. Forty minutes in and I was checking my phone every five minutes.
15. They took a great book and completely destroyed it. Embarrassing.
16. Not funny, not scary, not interesting. Just a waste of a cinema ticket.
17. The plot made no sense and the characters were completely unlikeable.
18. A cynical cash grab that insults the audience's intelligence.
19. I sat through two and a half hours of nothing. Infuriating.
20. The worst performance I've ever seen from such a talented actor. Shocking.

## Test Examples (For the Testing Protocol)

### Clearly Positive (Test Set A)

- "A delightful surprise — I laughed, I cried, I cheered."
- "The director's best work yet. Every scene was perfect."
- "My whole family loved it. We're watching it again this weekend."
- "An emotionally resonant film that stays with you long after the credits roll."
- "Genuinely moved by this one. Exceptional storytelling."
- "The kind of film you immediately want to tell everyone about."
- "Heart-pounding, tear-jerking, and jaw-dropping — all in one movie."
- "A triumph of modern cinema. Absolutely loved it."

### Clearly Negative (Test Set B)

- "I've seen better acting in a school play."
- "Two hours of my life I'll never get back."
- "The sequel nobody asked for, executed worse than expected."
- "A total disaster from start to finish. Avoid at all costs."
- "The characters made such dumb decisions I was laughing at the movie, not with it."
- "Derivative, dull, and deeply disappointing. What happened?"
- "The script felt like it was written in an afternoon. Painfully lazy."
- "Nothing about this film works. Not the story, the acting, or the music."

### Tricky / Ambiguous (Test Set C)

- "It's not the worst movie ever made, but it's close."
- "Some good moments buried under a lot of mediocrity."
- "Visually stunning but emotionally empty."
- "I didn't hate it."
- "Better than I expected, which isn't saying much."
- "Fine if you have nothing else to watch."
- "The first half was great; the second half ruined it."
- "An interesting failure — ambitious but deeply flawed."
- "My kids liked it. I tolerated it."
- "Competently made but completely forgettable."

---

## Notes for Teachers

The "tricky" reviews are intentionally designed to challenge students' thinking about what makes a review positive or negative. Discuss:

- Why is "I didn't hate it" hard to classify? (Double negative, low praise)
- Why is "Visually stunning but emotionally empty" hard? (Mixed sentiment)
- What does it reveal about how we use language when we're being ironic or lukewarm?

These examples are great for the post-lab discussion about AI limitations.
