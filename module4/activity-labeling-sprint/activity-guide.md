## What Is a Labeling Sprint?

Before an AI can learn to recognize emotions, **humans have to label the data**. This is called **data annotation** — and it's one of the most important (and underrated!) jobs in machine learning.

In this activity, you'll label 20 face images as either **HAPPY** or **NOT HAPPY**. Then you'll compare your labels with a partner to see how often humans agree — and explore what happens when they don't.

---
