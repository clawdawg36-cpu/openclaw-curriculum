# Data Audit Rubric — Module 4

**Module 4 | Machine Learning | OpenClaw Curriculum**

---

## Overview

Students document their training dataset before building their model. This rubric assesses the quality of their data audit.

## Rubric

| Criterion | 4 — Exceeds | 3 — Meets | 2 — Approaching | 1 — Beginning |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|-----------------|----------------|
| **Class Balance** | Documents exact counts per class; identifies imbalance; explains how imbalance affects training | Documents counts per class; notes any imbalance | Documents approximate counts; acknowledges imbalance if major | Minimal documentation of class counts |
| **Data Quality Assessment** | Identifies specific quality issues (lighting, angle, noise); explains impact on model | Identifies at least one quality issue | Mentions data quality generally | No quality assessment |
| **Bias Identification** | Identifies at least 2 potential biases in dataset; explains who might be harmed | Identifies 1 potential bias | Acknowledges bias exists but doesn't identify specific examples | No bias analysis |
| **Mitigation Plan** | Concrete plan with specific steps to address identified issues | General plan to improve dataset | Vague acknowledgment that improvement is needed | No mitigation plan |
