# Capability Unlock Reflection — Module 4

**Module 4 | Machine Learning | OpenClaw Curriculum**

---

## Prompt

Your agent can now use a model you trained. That's a real capability unlock — and it means part of your agent's brain is something you shaped.

Answer the following questions in 2-3 paragraphs:

1. **What did you train your model to do?** Describe the classification task you chose and why.

2. **What accuracy did you achieve?** What does that number mean in practice — if your model is 75% accurate, what does that feel like when you actually use it?

3. **Where did it fail?** Describe at least one specific case where your model got it wrong. Why do you think it made that mistake?

4. **What would you change about your training data?** If you could start over, what would you do differently?

5. **What does it mean that YOU trained this?** Your agent now has a capability that came from your design choices. How does that feel different from using a pre-built model?

## Rubric

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 4 | Addresses all 5 questions with specific examples; reflection shows genuine insight about the training process and its implications |
| 3 | Addresses all 5 questions; some specificity; reflection is honest and thoughtful |
| 2 | Addresses 3-4 questions; responses are somewhat generic |
| 1 | Addresses fewer than 3 questions or responses are very brief |
