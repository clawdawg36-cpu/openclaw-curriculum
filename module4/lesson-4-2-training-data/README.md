# Lesson 4.2 — Training Data: The Food AI Eats

**Module:** 4 — Give Your Agent the Ability to Learn  
**Duration:** 50–60 minutes  
**Grade Level:** 6–8

---

## Overview

Students explore the concept of training data — what it is, why it matters, and how it can go wrong. The key activity is a "Labeling Sprint" where students experience the real, human work of data labeling and discover firsthand how ambiguous or disagreed-upon labels affect model quality.

## Key Activity

**Labeling Sprint** — Three rounds: easy labels, ambiguous labels, and a cost/quality reveal. Students discover that labeling is harder than it looks, that humans disagree, and that those disagreements flow directly into model quality.

## Essential Questions

- What makes training data "good"?
- Who builds the world's training data, and at what cost?
- How does a biased dataset produce a biased model?
