# Lesson 4.4 — Overfitting and Model Evaluation

**Module:** 4 — Give Your Agent the Ability to Learn  
**Duration:** 50–60 minutes  
**Grade Level:** 6–8

---

## Overview

Students learn to distinguish between training accuracy and validation accuracy — and discover that high training accuracy can actually be a red flag. The "Memorizer vs. Generalizer" activity makes overfitting concrete, and students connect the concept back to their own Teachable Machine models from Lesson 4.3.

## Key Activity

**Memorizer vs. Generalizer** — Two classifiers with the same task but different training strategies. Classifier A: 100% training accuracy, 33% test accuracy (overfit). Classifier B: 87% training accuracy, 89% test accuracy (well-generalized). Students analyze the difference and connect it to their own lab work.

## Essential Questions

- What's the difference between memorizing training data and actually learning?
- Why is 100% training accuracy not necessarily a good sign?
- How do you tell if a model is ready to deploy?
