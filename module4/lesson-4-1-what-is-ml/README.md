# Lesson 4.1 — What Is Machine Learning?

**Module:** 4 — Give Your Agent the Ability to Learn  
**Duration:** 50–60 minutes  
**Grade Level:** 6–8

---

## Overview

Students are introduced to machine learning by contrasting it with traditional programming. The centerpiece is the "Human Learning Machine" game, where students role-play as a classifier — experiencing training, testing, and accuracy improvement firsthand.

## Key Activity

**Human Learning Machine Game** — Students act as a fruit classifier. Three rounds: baseline guess → training on labeled examples → testing on unseen data. Debrief connects directly to how ML models train.

## Essential Questions

- What's the difference between programming a computer and training one?
- What does "learning from data" actually mean?
- What happens when your training data is wrong?
