❓ Quick Answers

Common Questions

Is AI Explorers really free? ›
Yes — completely free for educators, schools, and homeschoolers. No hidden fees, no "freemium" wall, no paid upgrade required. The full curriculum is on GitHub under a CC BY-SA 4.0 license.
When will new modules be available? ›
Join the interest list and you'll be the first to know. We ship modules as they're ready — roughly one every 4–6 weeks during the development phase.
Can I pilot AI Explorers this semester? ›
Absolutely. Fill out the interest form above and check "I'm interested in piloting." We'll reach out to discuss your classroom setup, timeline, and how we can support you.
Do you offer professional development or training? ›
We're developing PD resources for educators. In the meantime, send us a message — we can often do a short async walkthrough for pilot schools.
