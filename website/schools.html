Privacy & Compliance

No student data collected. By design.

🏛 FERPA Compliant
👶 COPPA Compliant
🔒 No Student Accounts
📵 Zero Data Collection
🌐 No Third-Party Sharing

We collect no student data. The curriculum runs entirely in the browser with no backend data collection. There is no platform to sign students into, no data processing agreement required. Your district's student data never touches our infrastructure — because there is no infrastructure.

The curriculum files can be downloaded and served from your own district server, completely airgapped from any external system. You can verify every line of code in the open-source repository.

What data is collected

No student data whatsoever. Website analytics may collect aggregate, non-personal visit counts if you link to our hosted version. If self-hosted, zero data leaves your network.

Student safety

No chat interfaces, no external AI APIs accessed in student activities, no social features. All labs use curated, pre-reviewed datasets. No unpredictable AI outputs in student-facing content.

FERPA considerations

Because no education records are created or processed by the curriculum, FERPA's protections are not triggered. No DPA needed. No FERPA compliance audit required.

Can we get a DPA?

A data processing agreement is not required or applicable — there is no data processing. If your legal team needs documentation, we provide a one-page privacy summary for board records.
