Executive Summary

What Is AI Explorers?

AI Explorers is a free, open-source, project-based AI curriculum for middle school students. Rather than teaching students about artificial intelligence through lectures and readings, students build a working AI assistant across seven modules — adding one capability per module until they have a complete, named, personalized agent they can demo to a real audience.

The program targets grades 6–8, runs over one semester, and requires no prior coding experience from students or teachers. No budget. No vendor. No lock-in.

🎯 Why now? Districts that build AI literacy in the next 2–3 years will graduate students with a measurable advantage — not just tool familiarity, but the critical thinking and hands-on experience to evaluate, build, and responsibly deploy AI systems. AI Explorers is the most cost-effective path to that outcome available today.

Program Overview

Seven Modules. One Arc.

Every module answers one question: "What can your agent do now that it couldn't do before?" Each module closes with students updating their Agent Design Journal — a living record of what they built and why.
