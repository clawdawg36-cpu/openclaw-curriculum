## 🧠 The Setup

Right now, your personal agent lives in the world of text.

It can read. It can write. It can answer questions, make plans, and have conversations.  
But if you show it a photo? It needs **you** to describe it first.

That's about to change.

In **Lab 2.4**, you'll connect a vision skill to your agent. It will be able to:
- Look at an image and describe what it sees
- Answer questions about photos without you translating
- Spot details, read text in images, and identify objects directly

Before you get there — think about what that means for **your agent specifically**.

---

## ✏️ Your Task

Think about the agent you've been building this module. Its purpose. Its personality. What it's supposed to help with.

Now imagine it has **real eyes**.

**Write 3 use cases** — specific things your agent could do *with vision* that it can't do right now.

Be specific. "Help me more" is not a use case. "Look at a photo of my fridge and suggest what I can make for dinner" — that's a use case.
