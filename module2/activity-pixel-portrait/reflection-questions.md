## Quick Reference: Key Concepts

| Term | What It Means |
|:---|:---|
| **Pixel** | The smallest unit of a digital image — one colored square |
| **Resolution** | How many pixels an image has (more = more detail) |
| **RGB** | Red, Green, Blue — the three color channels that define each pixel's color |
| **Feature** | A pattern an AI uses to identify something (like "two dots near top = eyes") |
| **Training data** | Images the AI studied to learn what things look like |
| **Computer vision** | The field of AI that focuses on understanding images |

## For Discussion: The Big Question

> *"If a computer sees only numbers, and humans see meaning — what does it actually mean to 'understand' an image?"*

There's no right answer. But thinking about it will help you build better AI agents.
