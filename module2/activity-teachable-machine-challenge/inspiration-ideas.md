## 🏆 Bonus: What Makes These Ideas Win?

The best competition entries share a few traits:

- **You can demo it live** — picks something you can physically show
- **The classes are meaningfully different** — not "slightly different shades of the same thing"
- **It could actually be useful** — even if it's funny, you can explain *why* someone might want this
- **It surprises the audience** — "Wait, it can tell that?!"

---

## 🚫 Ideas to Avoid

These tend to be **hard to demo accurately** or **overdone**:

- ❌ Face recognition (raises privacy issues + hard to generalize)
- ❌ Cats vs. dogs (needs downloaded images, cliché)
- ❌ Color detection only (too simple, Teachable Machine isn't ideal for this)
- ❌ Generic "thumbs up / thumbs down / nothing" (everyone does this)
